March 6, 2020:

Travel Grants Available for GPU Hackathon

The San Diego Supercomputer Center (SDSC) will extend scholarships to team members who are Comet users interested in attending the GPU Hackathon. Qualifying team members are required to be active users of Comet, and demonstrate the need for funding to attend. Selected teams will be award up to $1000.00 to be used towards expenses associated with travel to the event, including; air, hotel, ground transportation and meals not covered by event. The scholarships are generously funded by the National Science Foundation (NSF Award #1341698) and its support of Comet education goals. Travel guidelines in accordance with the UC San Diego and NSF will apply.

 

Due to the COVID-19 issues, travel grant applications have been put on hold. Check back with in the future for other grant opportunities.
